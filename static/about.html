What is Image2Paste?

Image2Paste is a free web service that can convert text to image in a preferred style.
It is also an open source project hosted on github

Why Image2Paste?

-Avoid censorship: text is vulnerable to censorship(e.g. GFW), but image isn't
-Avoid crawlers/spammers: imaged text will be "invisible" to those robots
-Keep styles: imaged text is styled as is, it will not change in different web pages

Any limits on text size?

A single text is limited to under 500 lines due to performance concern, however you can convert whatever amount of text as you want.

How long will you keep the image?

The converted images will stick around forever.
